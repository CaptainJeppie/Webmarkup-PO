Iceland

Iceland has a beautiful and different environment it's such a destination that everyone wants to visit at least once. In this tour you will get to see the different natural occurances unique to the island. To have an absolute bathing experience you will travel to the Blue Lagoon and visit the Capital Reykjavik. The Golden Circle route is not missing with a tour through geisers, vulcano's, cliffs and ice cold waterfalls. With a little bit of luck you will get to see the Northern Light.
