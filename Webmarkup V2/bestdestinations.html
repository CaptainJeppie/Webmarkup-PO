Egypte

Egypte is a country of vast deserts on the continent of Africa. It has the Gulf of Aqaba and the Red Sea to the west. Egypt is officially known as Arab Republic of Egypt. There is also the Nile that formerly annually flooded but after building the Aswan Dam that stopped happening. As for the climate Egypt has an unusually hot, sunny and dry climate with cooling from the ocean winds.

Kaapverdië

Kaapverdië is a small island chain on the continent of Africa. it lies in the Atlantic Ocean. Kaapverdië is officially known as Repbuliek Kaapverdië. With an average temperature of 28 degrees Celsius. As for the climate Kaapverdië has a variaty in climates due to the difference in height of each island. The capital of Kaapverdië is Praia. Kaapverdië is a good place to learn how to windsurf because of the winds caused by the North-East Passaat.

Aruba

Aruba is a small on the Caribbean Sea on the continent of South-America. Aruba is officially known as Land Aruba, Pais Aruba. Aruba has a barren and rocky landscape with many cacti and the Dividivi trees. Due to the amount of rain being on average slightly lower than the average in the Netherlands, but due to the strong evaporation in this warm climate, and the concentration of rain in heavy showers Aruba remains arid and dry. The Dividivi is a tree characteristic of Aruba that grows with the wind.

Spain

Spain is a country with some islands on the South-West Europian continent. Spain was formerly known as Kingdom of Spain. Spain has islands in the Mediterranean Sea, the Canary islands in the Atlantic Ocean and some uninhabited islands. The Mediterranean climate, characterised by warm/hot and dry summers is dominant in the country of Spain. The capital of Spain is Madrid. If you like football Madrid has a popular footballclub called Real Madrid.

Greece

Greece is a Country with some islands on the south of the Europian continent. Greece is officially as the Hellenic Republic. Greece is considered the cradle of Western civilization, being the birthplace of democracy, Western philosophy, Western literature, historiography, political science, major scientific and mathematical principles, theatre and the Olympic Games. The capital of Greece is Athens. The climate of Greece is primarily Mediterranean, featuring mild, wet winters and hot, dry summers.

Portugal

Portugal is a small country next to Spain on the Europian continent. Portugal officially known as Portugese Republic. The climate of Portugal is primarily Mediterranean, featuring mild, wet winters and hot, dry summers. The capital of Portugal is Lisbon. Traditional architecture is distinctive and include the Manueline, also known as Portuguese late Gothic a sumptuous. The Portuguese consume a lot of dry cod, for which there are hundreds of recipes.
